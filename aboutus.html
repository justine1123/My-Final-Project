About Us

Our Mission The Office of the Controller at the University of Portland safeguards the University's assets; executes its financial transactions; ensures compliance with applicable laws and regulations; and provides accurate information, excellent customer service, and prudent business acumen to the University community in order to further the University's mission of teaching and learning; faith and formation; and service and leadership.
Our Vision The controller's office is a trusted source of information and advice. We are innovative, accurate, and thoughtful about our work. We collaborate well and advocate for the good of the University.
Our Values Consistent with the University of Portland our values include teaching and learning, faith and formation, and service and leadership. In addition, our department values include quality, integrity, and teamwork.
